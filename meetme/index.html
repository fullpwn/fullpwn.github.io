This fall, there’s more going on at the mall than back-to-school sales. Three special Meet Me tour trucks are criss-crossing the country, on their way to a mall in a city near you. They’re bringing a booth staffed with friendly experts who will give you a hands-on demonstration of Windows Me and answer your questions about Microsoft’s new operating system for home users.

You'll get personal, first-hand demonstrations of:

  • Digital video: Shoot your own video, edit it, add some music, and then send your personalized video e-mail to others using POPcast™.
  • Digital photography: Snap a picture of yourself at the beach or in front of the Eiffel Tower, and then share your simulated vacation photo with your friends via e-mail.
  • Digital music: See (and hear!) Windows Media™ Player 7 in action. Find out what Web radio, downloadable playlists, the Media Guide, and skins are all about.
  • Home networking: Test your reflexes with head-to-head gaming over networked computers, and watch a video (on a new plasma TV) showing an example home network in action.

There’s plenty more to see and experience, so come and meet Windows Me!
